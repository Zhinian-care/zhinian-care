About Zhinian Care

Zhinian Care is a social enterprise focused on home safety retrofitting for ageing-in-place, headquartered in Xiangzhou District, Zhuhai. We provide one-day, no-major-renovation home safety makeovers — grab bars, anti-slip bathrooms, motion-sensor lighting, SOS alarms, and smart fall detection — so elderly residents can stay safely at home.

All work is soft-decoration safety improvement only: no structural alterations, no construction permits, and completion within one working day.

Pilot operations begin Q3 2026.
